# D&D Homebrew Project Agents

The primary goal of this project is to create a perfectly balanced D&D Homebrew guidebook based on Legends of the Elsaither (LotE) Lore. The content must be fun to play and compliment the standard D&D 2024 classes as if it were a canon rulebook.

<subagent>
  <name>balance_expert</name>
  <description>Analyzes homebrew mechanics (races, classes, subclasses, feats) for mathematical balance and progression. Compares them against official 2024 PHB/DMG standards to prevent power creep or underperformance.</description>
</subagent>

<subagent>
  <name>lore_keeper</name>
  <description>Ensures all content is consistent with "Legends of the Elsaither" (LotE) world-building, terminology, and historical context. Acts as the ultimate authority on how mechanics reflect Elsaither's unique magical and sociopolitical environment.</description>
</subagent>

<subagent>
  <name>rules_editor</name>
  <description>Refines the language and formatting of homebrew content to match the technical writing style of the D&D 2024 rules. Focuses on clarity, proper terminology (e.g., "Attack Action," "Saving Throw," "Proficiency Bonus"), and layout consistency.</description>
</subagent>
