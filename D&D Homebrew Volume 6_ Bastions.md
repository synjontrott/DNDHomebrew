**Silveronian Bastions**

Special furnishings: 

Fusion Platform \- In the heart of the Nexus Forge, the Fusion Platform stands as a marvel of Silveronian design. Crafted from gleaming alloys and embedded with pulsating holographic displays, it exudes an otherworldly aura. Runes of light dance across its surface, guiding the intricate fusion process. At its center, a holographic matrix hovers, channeling elemental energies with precision. Surrounding the platform, advanced robotic arms move with fluid grace, assisting artisans in their work. The air hums with the sound of energy, creating an atmosphere of innovation and discovery. As the Fusion Platform hums to life, it becomes a beacon of progress, forging the future with every fusion cycle.

Plasma Forge \- In the heart of the Nexus Forge, the Plasma Forge stands as a testament to Silveronian technology and innovation. A large, metallic structure dominates the space, its sleek design complemented by glowing plasma conduits running along its surface. The air crackles with energy as streams of plasma flow through intricate channels, creating an ethereal display of light and color. At its center, a circular platform hovers above the ground, surrounded by arrays of precision instruments and control panels. Jets of plasma shoot out from nozzles, shaping molten metal with incredible precision. Workers clad in protective gear move with purpose, harnessing the power of plasma to craft advanced armor and weapons with unparalleled strength and durability. The atmosphere hums with the sounds of machinery and energy, as the Plasma Forge continues its ceaseless work at the forefront of technological advancement.

**Special Facilities**

Nexus Forge  
Level 5 Bastion Facility  
Prerequisite: Silveronian Race or Elemental Warrior Class  
Space: Roomy  
Hirelings: 2  
Order: Craft (1d4 BP)

This Nexus Forge contains a fusion platform, a plasma forge, and other tools needed to craft weapons and tools.

When you issue the Craft order to this facility, choose one of the following options:

Craft: Ammunition or Simple Weapon. You commission the facility’s hirelings to craft up to two fusion matrices of any element, or one simple weapon, which takes 7 days and costs you half the normal price of the ammunition or weapon. Or you can commission a Silveronian weapon to be made, of uncommon rarity or lower, found in magical and non magical items, which takes 14 days and costs half the normal price.

Craft: Armor or Equipment. You commission the facility’s hirelings to craft one of the following: a suit of Standard Silveronian Armor, Silveronian Juggernaut Armor, 20 caltrops, a chain (10 feet), a crowbar, a grappling hook, a hammer (regular or sledge), 4 horseshoes, a hunting trap, a set of power-resistant manacles, a miner’s pick, 10 iron spikes or pitons, an iron pot, a metal shield, or a shovel. The work takes 7 days, or 21 days for a suit of armor, and costs you half the item’s normal cost.

Craft: Martial Weapon. You commission the facility’s hirelings to craft a martial weapon, which takes 14 days and costs you half the weapon’s normal cost, or a rare Silveronian weapon, which takes 21 days and costs half the weapon’s normal cost.

Side note: Some Silveronian weapons and armor require power crystals or crystalarium crystals, which are consumed during the crafting process.

Crystal Garden  
Level 9 Bastion Facility  
Prerequisite: Silveronian Race or Elemental Warrior Class  
Space: Roomy  
Hirelings: 1  
Order: Harvest (1d6 BP)

A Crystal Garden is a spacious enclosure in which various crystalline plants can be grown in a controlled climate.

When you issue the Harvest order to this facility, choose one of the following options:

Harvest: Crystalarium. You commission the facility’s hireling to harvest a single Crystalarium plant with five crystals, or you do the work yourself. The work takes 21 days and costs nothing.

Harvest: Power Crystal. You commission the facility’s hireling to harvest a single power crystal, its element being your choice. You may also roll on the elemental table to help you decide. The work takes 14 days and costs nothing.

Beast Training Complex  
Level 13 Bastion Facility  
Prerequisite: Silveronian Race or Elemental Warrior Class  
Space: Vast  
Hirelings: 2  
Order: Recruit (1d8 BP)

A beast training complex keeps various creatures in enclosures and is where they are trained and tamed for protection. When you issue the Recruit order to this facility, you commission the facility’s hirelings to add a creature, either a Meite, a Vortzhe, or a Xelis, to the facility. The recruitment takes 14 days and costs 2,000 cc. The hirelings make sure the creature is properly looked after. The training complex is large enough to accommodate two of these creatures. Keep track of the creatures housed in your training complex, as they count as bastion defenders. Deduct any you lose from your defenders roster.

Zerospace Engineering Hub  
Level 17 Bastion Facility  
Prerequisite: Silveronian Race or Elemental Warrior Class  
Space: Vast  
Hirelings: 10  
Order: Craft (1d10 BP)

A Zerospace Engineering Hub comes with a large facility designed to craft and house starships of Silveronian design. It is a unique and expansive area that can house up to two starships of up to 7 tons each, which come with a specified number of pilots minus the main pilot, which is your character. When you issue the Craft order, you commission the hirelings to build one of the vehicles in the Silveronian vehicles list. You pay the full cost of the vehicle, and the work takes 1 day per 1,000 cc of the vehicle’s cost (an X-blade can be built in 10 days). You are responsible for the fuel of the vehicle, though you can also issue the Craft order to create bombs and missiles at full price, which takes 7 days each, and you can simultaneously create up to 1 armament per available hireling.
