# **Deity Based Abilities**

# **Pireous (Destruction)**

Fire (Cantrip)  
Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 30 feet  
Duration: Instantaneous

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn't being worn or carried.

# **Firena (Healing)**

Fire (Cantrip)  
Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 60 feet  
Duration: Instantaneous

You create a bonfire of healing light. Choose a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d8 radiant damage. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw.

# **Hydronimus (Power)**

Water (Cantrip)  
Supportive  
Casting Time: 1 action    
Range: 60 feet     
Duration: Instantaneous

You unleash a blast of pressurized water at a creature you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 bludgeoning damage and if it is Large or smaller, it is pushed 5 feet away from you.

# **Riviera (Life)**

Water (Cantrip)  
Supportive  
Casting Time: 1 action    
Range: 30 feet     
Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a splash of healing water. A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains 1d4 hit points. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

# **Caelum (Power)**

Air (Cantrip)  
Supportive    
Casting Time: 1 action    
Range: 60 feet    
Duration: Instantaneous

You create a sudden burst of wind. A creature you choose within range must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take 1d6 bludgeoning damage and be pushed 10 feet away from you.

# **Anima (Balance \- Life and Death)**

# **Fulgur (Chaos)**

Lightning (Cantrip)  
Supportive    
Casting Time: 1 action    
Range: 120 feet    
Duration: Instantaneous

You create a lash of lightning energy that strikes at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 lightning damage. On a hit, you can pull the target 10 feet closer to you.

# **Fulmena (Order/Power)**

Lightning (Cantrip)  
Supportive    
Casting Time: 1 action    
Range: Touch    
Duration: Instantaneous

You touch a creature, and lightning shocks them. Make a melee spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 lightning damage, and it can't take reactions until the start of its next turn.

# **Mundus (Harmony/Flora)**

# **Terra (Protection/Fauna)**

Earth (Cantrip)  
Supportive

Range: 30 feet
