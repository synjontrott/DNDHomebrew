**Deity Based Abilities**

**Pireous (Destruction)**

Raze   
Level 8 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 150 feet  
Duration: Instant  

A surge of fiery energy erupts from your outstretched hands, engulfing the chosen area in flames. Each creature in a 60-foot cube centered on the point chosen must make a DEX save. On a fail, a creature takes **12d12 fire damage**, and half on a success. Structures in the area take double damage.

Molten Embrace   
Level 6 Defensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self  
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You use your powers to transform your skin into molten lava, granting you immunity from fire damage and thermal effects. The intense heat radiating from your skin imposes disadvantage on attack rolls of creatures within a 10-foot radius, and they take 2d6 fire damage at the start of their turn.

Volcanic Eruption   
Level 8 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 150 feet  
Duration: Instantaneous

Choose a point within range. Molten lava erupts from the ground in a 20-foot-radius cylinder and extends upward, damaging everything in its area. Each creature in that area must make a DEX save. A creature takes **10d8 fire damage and 10d8 bludgeoning damage** on a failed save, or half on a success.
The eruption leaves behind difficult terrain as the ground becomes uneven with cooled lava, dealing 2d6 fire damage to any creature that enters the area for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there. The lava cools after 1 minute.

**Firena (Healing)**

Flame’s Blessing   
Level 5 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You touch a willing creature and infuse it with your elemental energy, granting them Flame’s Blessing. For the duration, the target gains the following benefits:

1. Fire Empowerment \- The creature’s attacks deal an additional 1d6 fire damage  
2. Thermal Endurance \- The creature gains resistance to fire damage  
3. Survivor’s Flame \- The creature can withstand extreme heat environments, as if they were a fire elemental

Maintaining this connection requires the caster’s concentration.

**Umbra (Raw Darkness)**

Shadow Whisper  
2nd-level Supportive  
Casting Time: 1 action  
Range: 120 feet  
Components: V, S (The verbal component is the whispered message in the Shadow Tongue)  
Duration: Instantaneous

You tap into the resonant frequencies of the Shadow Realm to deliver a message that bypasses normal auditory limitations. This spell requires the ability to speak the Shadow Tongue.

You whisper a message of up to 25 words in the Shadow Tongue. This whisper itself serves as the spell's verbal component and can be cast even within an area of magical silence, its power originating from a source other than sound. You can deliver this whisper in one of two ways:

• In Dreams: You target a single sleeping or unconscious creature within range. The message manifests clearly within their mind.

• In Silence: You target a point you can see within range that is inside an area of magical silence. The whisper emanates from that point, audible to creatures within 10 feet of it.

The whisper is incomprehensible and terrifying to any creature that does not understand the Shadow Tongue. Every creature of your choice that hears the whisper (and does not understand the language) must make a Wisdom saving throw.

• On a failed save, a creature takes 3d6 psychic damage and is frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

• On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and is not frightened.

Creatures who understand the Shadow Tongue are not required to make a saving throw; they simply hear the message you intended and suffer no ill effects.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the psychic damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

Additionally, when you use a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional sleeping or unconscious creature for every two slot levels above 2nd (two targets at 4th level, three at 6th, and four at 8th).
