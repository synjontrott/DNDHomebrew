# **Abilities List**

Abilities work as standard spells. The Savaroen spell list defines which spells you can prepare. Tier 1 spells are 1st-level spells, Tier 2 are 3rd-level spells, Tier 3 are 6th-level spells, and Tier 4 are 9th-level spells. You can cast these spells using slots of the appropriate level or higher.

# **Cantrips**

Energy Shield  
Defensive Cantrip  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self  
Duration: Start of your next turn

You create a shield of energy around yourself. You gain 1d4 temporary hit points until the start of your next turn.
The temp hp increases by 1d4 at 5th (2d4), 11th (3d4), and 17th (4d4) levels.

Beam  
Offensive Cantrip  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 120 feet  
Duration: Instant

You fire a beam of elemental energy at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 elemental damage.
The spell creates more than one beam when you reach higher levels: two beams at 5th level, three beams at 11th level, and four beams at 17th level. You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.

Wound Wrap  
Supportive Cantrip  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Instant

You touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable. If the creature is conscious, you can instead grant it 1 temporary hit point. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

# **Tier 1 Abilities (1st Level)**

Orb  
Level 1 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 90 feet  
Duration: Instant

You hurl a 4-inch-diameter sphere of energy at a creature that you can see within range. Choose one target, or choose two targets within 5 feet of each other. Make a ranged spell attack against the target(s). On a hit, the target takes 3d8 elemental damage.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Lash  
Level 1 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self (15-foot cone)  
Duration: Instant

A wave of elemental energy erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 elemental damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Shield Wall  
Level 1 Defensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 15-feet  
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Creates a flat wall of energy 20 feet long, 8 feet high, and 1 foot thick. The wall provides three-quarters cover. It has AC 10 and 30 hit points. It is immune to poison and psychic damage. 
The hp increases by 10hp for every slot above 1st used in casting

Flesh Wound  
Level 1 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Instant

A creature you touch regains a number of hit points equal to 1d8 \+ your Spellcasting Ability Modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

# **Tier 2 Abilities (3rd Level)**

Circular Pulse  
Level 3 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 150 feet  
Duration: Instant

A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range then blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of elemental energy. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 8d6 elemental damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

Armor Invigoration  
Level 3 Defensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

You touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor, or is wearing light armor. Until the spell ends, the target's AC increases by 2.

Vanguard’s Manifestation  
Level 3 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self  
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You conjure a shimmering, translucent shield on one arm and an elemental weapon of your choice in your other hand. Both are formed from solidified elemental energy.

For the duration, you gain the following benefits:
* **Arcane Shield:** You gain a **\+2 bonus to AC**. This bonus does not stack with the bonus provided by a physical shield.
* **Spectral Armament:** The conjured weapon counts as a magical simple or martial weapon with which you are proficient. It deals **2d8 \+ your Charisma Ability Modifier** in elemental damage on a hit.

Boost  
Level 3 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Choose a willing creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, the target's speed is doubled, it gains a +2 bonus to AC, it has advantage on Dexterity saving throws, and it gains an additional action on each of its turns. That action can be used only to take the Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object action.

When the spell ends, the target can't move or take actions until after its next turn, as a wave of lethargy sweeps over it.

# **Tier 3 Abilities (6th Level)**

Sphere  
Level 6 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 150 feet  
Duration: Instant

You create a massive sphere of destructive energy. Each creature in a 60-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 8d8 elemental damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Catch and Absorb  
Level 6 Defensive  
Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when you take damage of your elemental type  
Range: Self  
Duration: 1 round

You have resistance to the triggering damage type until the start of your next turn. Also, the first time you hit with a melee attack on your next turn, the target takes an extra 4d6 damage of that type.

Temporal Shift  
Level 6 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self  
Duration: Up to 8 hours

You step into the border regions of the Ethereal Plane, in the area where it overlaps with your current plane. You remain in the Border Ethereal for the duration or until you use your action to dismiss the spell. During this time, you can move in any direction. If you move up or down, every foot of movement costs an extra foot. You can see and hear the plane you originated from, but everything there looks gray, and you can't see anything more than 60 feet away. While on the Ethereal Plane, you can only affect and be affected by other creatures on that plane. Creatures that aren't on the Ethereal Plane can't perceive you and can't interact with you, unless a special ability or magic has given them the ability to do so.

# **Tier 4 Abilities (9th Level)**

Hail  
Level 9 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: 1 mile  
Duration: Instant

Blazing orbs of fire, ice, or energy plummet to the ground at four different points you can see within range. Each creature in a 40-foot-radius sphere centered on each point you choose must make a Dexterity saving throw. The sphere spreads around corners. A creature takes 20d6 elemental damage and 20d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature in the area of more than one fiery burst is affected only once.

Three Point Beam  
Level 9 Offensive  
Casting time: 1 action  
Range: Self (300-foot line)  
Duration: Instant

You fire a massive beam of energy in a line that is 300 feet long and 15 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 40d6 elemental damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If this damage reduces a creature to 0 hit points, it is disintegrated.

Resurrection  
Level 9 Supportive  
Casting time: 1 hour  
Range: Touch  
Duration: Instant

You touch a creature that has been dead for no longer than 200 years and that died for any reason except old age. If the creature's soul is free and willing, the creature is restored to life with all its hit points. This spell closes all wounds, neutralizes any poison, cures all diseases, and lifts any curses affecting the creature when it died. The spell replaces damaged or missing organs and limbs. The spell requires a **Crystalarium Plant** which is consumed by the spell.

# **Feats**

## **Origin Feats**

Violent Soldier  
Origin Feat

You’ve been trained to deal particularly powerful attacks.
**Great Weapon Mastery:** When you roll a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack you make with a melee weapon or spell attack, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll, even if the new roll is a 1 or a 2.

Skilled  
Origin Feat

You gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice.
Repeatable. You can take this feat more than once.

Wilderness Brawler  
Origin Feat

You gain the following benefits.
* **Elemental Unarmed Strike.** When you hit with your unarmed strike and deal damage, you can deal elemental damage equal to 1d4 plus your charisma modifier instead of the normal damage of an unarmed strike.
* **Improvised Weaponry.** You have proficiency with improvised weapons. 
* **Push.** When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike as part of the attack action on your turn, you can deal damage to the target and also push it 5 feet away from you. You can use this benefit only once per turn.

Smithy  
Origin Feat

You gain the following benefits.
* **Tool Proficiency.** You gain proficiency with the artisan’s tools from the Elemental Smithy table.
* **Discount.** Whenever you buy a nonmagical item, you receive a 20 percent discount on it.
* **Elemental Smithy.** When you finish a long rest, you can craft one piece of gear from the Elemental Smithy table, provided you have the artisan’s tools associated with that item and have proficiency with those tools. The item lasts until you finish another long rest, at which point the item falls apart.

Alert  
Origin Feat

You gain the following benefits
* **Initiative Proficiency.** When you roll initiative, you can add your proficiency bonus to the roll.
* **Initiative Swap.** Immediately after you roll initiative, you can swap your initiative with the initiative of one willing ally in the same combat. You can’t make this swap if you or the ally has the incapacitated condition.

Tough  
Origin Feat

Your Hit Point Maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a character level thereafter, your Hit Point Maximum increases by an additional 2 HP.

Healing  
Origin Feat

You gain the following benefits.
* **Elemental Medic.** You infuse an elemental wound wrap (if you have one) with elemental energy and tend to a creature within 5 feet of yourself as a utilize action. That creature can expend one of its hit point dice, and you then roll that die. The creature regains a number of hit points equal to the roll plus your proficiency bonus.
* **Healing rerolls.** Whenever you roll a die to determine the number of hit points you restore with a spell or with this feat’s elemental medic benefit, you can reroll the die if it rolls a 1, and you must use the new roll.

# **Elemental Specialties**

At 7th level, you choose one of the following specializations:

**Option A: Animal Spirit**
You can manifest a spirit companion.
*   **Passive:** You gain proficiency in **Animal Handling** (or expertise if already proficient).
*   **Active:** As an Action, you can expend a use of your *Elemental Surge* to summon your **Spirit Companion** (use *Summon Beast* statistics from Tasha’s Cauldron, but it deals your elemental damage type). It lasts for 1 hour, requires Concentration, and acts on your initiative.

**Option B: Hiarelobi (Dual Element)**
Your dormant second element awakens.
*   **Dual Nature:** Choose a second element. You gain **Resistance** to this element’s primary damage type.
*   **Adaptive Casting:** When you cast a Savaroen spell that deals elemental damage, you can choose for it to deal the damage type of your **Secondary Element** instead of your Primary one.
*   **Fusion Blast (1/Long Rest):** When you cast a spell that deals damage, you can combine both elements. The spell deals maximum damage instead of rolling.
