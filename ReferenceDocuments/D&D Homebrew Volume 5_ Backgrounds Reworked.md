**Backgrounds**

**Elemental Warrior**

You were taken from your home when you were young and brought to a training facility on your home planet to learn how to fight. War is all you have ever known. You have spent your life, up until now, training for combat and nothing more. Sometimes you catch yourself reflexively performing the basic fighting exercises you learned first. Now, the time has come to put that training to use on the battlefield, protecting your home in the war that rages on.

Ability Scores: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution  
Feat: Violent Soldier  
Skill Proficiencies: Athletics and Intimidation  
Tool Proficiency: choose one kind of gaming set  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Greatsword, Longbow, 20 Arrows, Gaming Set (Same as above), Healer’s Kit, Quiver, Standard Underclothes, 12cc; or (B) 50cc

**Elemental Noble**

You grew up in a large, elegant, castle of a home, and your parents had you trained for combat. You are well acquainted with most areas of combat expertise, whether that is you understanding them or actually participating. You have learned a great deal about leadership, as well as the concepts of war.

Ability Scores: Dexterity, Charisma, Intelligence  
Feat: Skilled  
Skill Proficiencies: History and Persuasion  
Tool Proficiency: Choose one musical instrument or gaming set  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Musical Instrument or Gaming Set (same as above), Regal Underclothes, Power Crystal (matching your element), 25cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Wanderer**

You have wandered the lands and connected deeply with your element for as long as you can remember. You learned survival techniques, the use of weapons and armor, and the basics of battlefield training by being out in the field, learning firsthand. You might have run away from home when you were young, or perhaps after basic training you decided to venture out on your own and connect with nature directly.

Ability Scores: Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom  
Feat: Wilderness Brawler  
Skill Proficiencies: Religion and Survival  
Tool Proficiency: Gatherer’s Tools  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Glaive, Mace, Gatherer’s Tools, Tent, Standard Underclothes, 2cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Smith**

You grew up as an apprentice in a smith’s shop, hearing constant stories of war and epic battles, some mild, others gruesome. You learned to create basic crafts, as well as how to sweet talk customers. Your trade has given you a keen eye for detail, and a soft heart for those that were lost in combat to your customers and friends.

Ability Scores: Strength, Constitution, Wisdom  
Feat: Smithy  
Skill Proficiencies: Persuasion and Investigation  
Tool Proficiency: Fusion Smith’s Tools  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Warhammer, Fusion Smith’s Tools, 2 Pouches, Standard Underclothes, 27cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Traitor**

You found out early that you fought for the wrong side, but not early enough. You’ve done things you can’t undo. Seen what you can’t unsee. Your past still haunts you, but your future gives you hope that everything will be ok. You still have some bad habits that you picked up from your old allies, which sometimes serve you well, and sometimes get you into trouble, but you are always working to better yourself.

Ability Scores: Dexterity, Charisma, Constitution  
Feat: Alert  
Skill Proficiencies: Sleight of Hand and Stealth  
Tool Proficiency: Forgery Kit  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) 2 Daggers, Forgery Kit, 2 Pouches, Standard Underclothes, 14cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Farmer**

You grew up close to the land. Years tending animals and cultivating the ground rewarded you with patience and good health. You have a keen appreciation for nature’s bounty alongside a healthy respect for its wrath.

Ability Scores: Strength, Constitution, Wisdom  
Feat: Tough  
Skill Proficiencies: Animal Handling and Nature  
Tool Proficiency: Carpenter’s Tools  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Sickle, Carpenter’s Tools, Healer’s Kit, Iron Pot, Shovel, Standard Underclothes, 28cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Guard**

Your feet ache when you remember the countless hours you spent at your post on the walls, watchtowers, and the master’s quarters. You were trained to keep one eye looking outside the wall, watching for the enemy coming from the forests, and your other eye looking inside the wall, searching for spies and traitors.

Ability Scores: Strength, Intelligence, Wisdom  
Feat: Alert  
Skill Proficiencies: Athletics and Perception  
Tool Proficiency: Choose one kind of gaming set  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Spear, Longbow, 20 Arrows, Gaming Set (Same as above), Duchina Restraints, Standard Underclothes, Quiver, 10cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Hermit**

You spent your early years away from the rest of society, living in a nicely renovated cave or hut located in the middle of nowhere. In those days, your only companions were the creatures of the forest and others from your group that would occasionally bring information and goods from distant towns and bases. The solitude allowed you to spend many hours pondering the mysteries of creation and your role in the war.

Ability Scores: Constitution, Wisdom, Charisma  
Feat: Healing  
Skill Proficiencies: Religion and Insight  
Tool Proficiency: Herbalism Kit  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Quarterstaff, Dagger, Herbalism Kit, Book (Hokosha), Standard Underclothes, 16cc; or (B) 50cc 

**Elemental Tinkerer**

You spent your young years playing with technology, taking things apart, putting them together in new and exciting ways, “inventing” new uses for things around the house. Eventually, you put these skills to work in terms of weapons, armor, and other useful combat apparatus. You haven’t ventured far from your tinkering past, and still use your knowledge to figure out how things work, what makes them tick, and how it can be improved.

Ability Scores: Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma  
Feat: Smithy  
Skill Proficiencies: Arcana and Insight  
Tool Proficiency: Tinker’s Tools  
Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Dagger, Duchina Restraints, Tinker’s Tools, Standard Underclothes, 18cc; or (B) 50cc
