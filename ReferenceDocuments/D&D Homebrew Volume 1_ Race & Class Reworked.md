## **Krenpowen/Tenebrie (Ninjonian Cluster)**

Krenpowen/Tenebrie (Ninjonian Cluster) Traits

Creature type: Humanoid  
Size: Medium (about 5-7 feet tall)  
Speed: 30 feet

Your Krenpowen or Tenebrie character has a variety of natural abilities, the result of yons of training.

Age \- Because of their home planet’s slow orbit and rotation, Krenpowen and Tenebrie age slower than other races, living for 1000 years or more. A Krenpowen or Tenebrie will not reach maturity until they have reached ten times the human average.

Elementally charged \- You can choose your element at character creation. This affects most abilities cast by this race. You are allowed to choose Fire, Water, Ice, Earth, Air, Lightning, Laser, Gas, Metal, Bone, Shadow, Death, Poison, Knife, and Darki. The last four are Tenebrie or Nidosian only. When you cast spells using this class, your attacks automatically do your primary damage type. If you so choose, you may roll a D4 before any elemental damage roll and choose the corresponding damage type from your element. You may also choose your damage type from the same list before you cast a spell, allowing you to strategically choose rather than trust the wild magic. Your opposing element gives you vulnerability according to its primary damage type, while your inferior element grants you resistance against its primary damage type. Primary and secondary damage types can be found in the elements table. This trait also allows you to use cantrips of your chosen element from the Savaroen spells list.

Deep Focus \- You don’t always need sleep. Instead, you can meditate for 4 hours a day. While meditating, you gain the same benefit that a human does from an 8 hour sleep, as meditation communicates with the spirit realm. After meditating, you also have 10 minutes of ethereal sight, though if your meditation is interrupted before the 4 hours elapse, you do not gain the benefits.

Krenpowen/Tenebrie \- You have proficiency in the Performance, Intimidation, or Deception skills

### **Core Abilities**

**Spellcasting**
You have learned to draw upon your power spirit to cast spells.
*   **Cantrips:** You know 3 cantrips of your choice from the Savaroen spell list. You learn additional cantrips at higher levels, as shown in the Cantrips Known column of the Savaroen table.
*   **Preparing and Casting Spells:** The Savaroen table shows how many spell slots you have to cast your spells of 1st level and higher. To cast one of these spells, you must expend a slot of the spell’s level or higher. You regain all expended spell slots when you finish a long rest.
*   **Prepared Spells:** You prepare the list of Savaroen spells that you are available to cast, choosing from the Savaroen spell list. When you do so, choose a number of Savaroen spells equal to your Charisma modifier \+ your Savaroen level (minimum of one spell). The spells must be of a level for which you have spell slots.
*   **Ritual Casting:** You can cast a Savaroen spell as a ritual if that spell has the ritual tag and you have the spell prepared.
*   **Spellcasting Ability:** Charisma is your spellcasting ability for your Savaroen spells.
    *   **Spell save DC** \= 8 \+ your proficiency bonus \+ your Charisma modifier
    *   **Spell attack modifier** \= your proficiency bonus \+ your Charisma modifier

**Elemental Surge**
Starting at 2nd level, you can channel your element to strike with greater force. As a **Bonus Action**, you can imbue your strikes with power for 1 minute.
*   **Effect:** The first time you hit with a weapon attack or spell attack on each of your turns, you deal an extra **1d6** damage of your chosen elemental type.
*   **Usage:** You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**. You regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

**Elemental Philosophy**
At 2nd level, you adopt a philosophy that grants you a **Channel Divinity**-like ability. You can use this feature **once per Short or Long Rest**.

*   **Creation:** As an Action, you can touch a creature to channel restorative energy. They regain hit points equal to **2d8 \+ your Charisma modifier**. This healing increases to 3d8 at 5th level, 4d8 at 10th level, and 5d8 at 15th level.
*   **Destruction:** When you roll damage for a Savaroen spell, you can reroll any 1s or 2s on the damage dice. You must use the new rolls.
*   **Balance:** As a Reaction when you or an ally within 30 feet takes damage, you can reduce that damage by **1d10 \+ your Savaroen level**.

**Subclass**
At 3rd level, you choose a subclass that represents your specific training style: Ninjonian, Ninjarikan, Ninji, Techno, Tek, Technika, or Warrion. Your choice grants you features at 3rd level and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th level.

**Spiritual Resonance**
At 5th level, your connection to your power spirit deepens. Whenever you expend a Spell Slot of 3rd level or higher, you gain **Temporary Hit Points** equal to the **Slot Level \+ your Charisma modifier**.

**Elemental Specialty**
At 7th level, you choose one of the following specializations to further refine your power:

*   **Option A: Animal Spirit**
    *   **Passive:** You gain proficiency in Animal Handling (or expertise if already proficient).
    *   **Active:** As an Action, you can expend a use of your *Elemental Surge* to summon your **Spirit Companion** (use *Summon Beast* statistics from Tasha’s Cauldron, but it deals your elemental damage type). It lasts for 1 hour, requires Concentration, and acts on your initiative.
*   **Option B: Hiarelobi (Dual Element)**
    *   **Dual Nature:** Choose a second element. You gain **Resistance** to this element’s primary damage type.
    *   **Adaptive Casting:** When you cast a Savaroen spell that deals elemental damage, you can choose for it to deal the damage type of your **Secondary Element** instead of your Primary one.
    *   **Fusion Blast (1/Long Rest):** When you cast a spell that deals damage, you can combine both elements. The spell deals maximum damage instead of rolling.

**Improved Surge**
At 11th level, the extra damage from your *Elemental Surge* increases to **1d8**.

**Transcendent Spirit**
At 18th level, your physical form is suffused with raw power.
*   **Stat Increase:** Your **Charisma** and **Constitution** scores increase by **2**. Your maximum for these scores is now **22**.
*   **Legacy:** You stop aging and cannot be aged magically.

**The Power Within**
At 20th level, you can manifest the ultimate expression of your power: a **Divine Elemental Weapon**.
*   **Activation:** Bonus Action.
*   **Duration:** 1 minute (Once per Long Rest).
*   **Form:** A weapon made of pure energy appears in your hand (or floats near you). It counts as a Simple Melee Weapon with which you are proficient. It uses your **Charisma** modifier for attack and damage rolls.
*   **Damage:** On a hit, it deals **4d10 \+ Charisma modifier** of your elemental damage type.
*   **Properties:**
    *   This weapon ignores Resistance and Immunity.
    *   When you take the Attack action with this weapon, you can make **two attacks** with it (regardless of your subclass).
    *   While the weapon is active, you have **Resistance to all damage**.

### **Subclasses**

#### **Ninji (The Heavy Artillery)**
*Vision: A stationary turret of overwhelming elemental firepower.*

*   **Level 3: Elemental Bypass**
    Spells you cast ignore **Resistance** to your chosen elemental damage type. If a creature has **Immunity** to your element, it is treated as Resistance instead.
*   **Level 6: Potent Spellcasting**
    When you cast a Savaroen spell that deals damage of your chosen elemental type, you can add your **Charisma modifier** to one damage roll of that spell.
*   **Level 10: Echoes of Power**
    When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher that targets only one creature and deals damage, you can target a second creature within 10 feet of the first. The second creature takes half damage (no save).
*   **Level 14: Elemental Cataclysm**
    You become a conduit of raw destruction. You gain the following benefits:
    *   **Overchannel:** When you cast a spell of 1st through 5th level that deals damage of your elemental type, you can deal maximum damage with that spell instead of rolling. You can use this feature once per Long Rest.
    *   **Siege Monster:** Your spells deal double damage to objects and structures.

#### **Ninjonian (The Custom Martial)**
*Vision: A highly skilled weapon master who relies on technique over raw rage.*

*   **Level 3: Combat Superiority**
    *   **Bonus Proficiencies:** Martial Weapons, Shields, Acrobatics Skill.
    *   **Fighting Style:** You gain **one Fighting Style Feat** of your choice (e.g., Two-Weapon Fighting, Great Weapon Fighting, Duelist, Archery).
*   **Level 6: Extra Attack**
    You can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.
*   **Level 10: Evasion**
    When you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, and only half damage if you fail.
*   **Level 14: Whirlwind Strike**
    You can use your Action to make a melee attack against any number of creatures within 5 feet of you, with a separate attack roll for each target.

*   **Level 6: War Magic**

*   **Level 10: Eldritch Strike**

# **Half Race Rules**

Ability score increase  
Take one score increase from mother and one from father, one major and one minor increase

Age  
Take the age of the parent who has a shorter lifespan

Alignment  
Half-races tend towards chaotic, but Krenpowen will have a lean towards good, and Tenebrie will have a lean towards evil, but if the character takes on the lawfulness of Silveronia, they will almost certainly be Lawful Neutral.

Size  
Average the expected height and weight based on the two parent races

Speed  
Speed mostly depends on size. Medium is 30, small is 25

Languages  
You will know common and most likely the languages known by the parents that raised you. Don’t expect to know the language of an absent parent. Unless it is an exotic inborn language like draconic from Dragonborn or infernal from tieflings.
